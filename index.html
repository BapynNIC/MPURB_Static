About MPURB

The State Government shall constitute a Regulatory Board consisting of a Chairman and three Members nominated from among eminent educationists and experienced administrators.

The Board shall undertake the following responsibilities:

  1. To periodically inspect all Private Universities which have been set up in Meghalaya including study centre etc.
  2. (b) to requisition any specific information from the institutions indicated at (a) above
  3. To call for reports from Directorate of Higher and Technical Education or directly from the Registrar of the concerned University hich may be of academic nature and otherwise
  4. To ensure that quality education is imparted by these institutions as per the guidelines of the UGC or any Statutory Body /Regulatory Body under the control of Government
  5. Maintenance of permanent headquarters at Shillong.
  6. Regulation of honorarium and allowances of members.
  7. Submission of Annual Reports with recommendations.
  8. Reporting major institutional developments to Government.
  9. The Regulatory Board shall have a permanent office with adequate staff with Headquarters at Shillong:
  10. The members shall be entitled to such Honorarium, travelling allowances and daily allowances as shall be prescribed separately
  11. The Regulatory Board shall submit Annual Reports in respect of the Private Universities including those Universities having off campus centre/study centre/regional centre etc. functioning in the State. Such reports shall make specific recommendations for action/s to be taken by the Government including imposition of penalty as provided for any violation of relevant provisions laid down by Government; and
  12. The Board shall bring any important development in respect of any Private University as it arises, to the notice of the Government.

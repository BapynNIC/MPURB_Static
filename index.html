Est. by Act of State Legislature

Meghalaya Private University Regulatory Board

The objective of the MPURB is to regulate the standards of Private Universities established by the Act of the State Legislature of Meghalaya. We ensure compliance, inspection, and the maintenance of academic excellence.

Recognized Universities Our Mandate

Mandate & Functions

As defined by the Terms of Reference of the Board

Periodic Inspection

Inspecting all Private Universities and study centers set up in Meghalaya.

Information Requisition

Calling for academic and administrative reports from Registrars.

UGC Compliance

Ensuring quality education aligns with UGC and Statutory Body guidelines.

Regulatory Action

Recommending penalties to the Government for violations of provisions.
